How to Enroll

  1. Open web browser.
  2. Click here to enroll.
  3. In the dashboard, select "NEW STUDENT" for incoming grade 7 students and transferees. Select "OLD STUDENT" for students who were enrolled in the last school year.
  4. Fill out the enrollment form and do not leave any items unanswered especially the HOUSEHOLD CAPACITY AND ACCESS DISTANCE LEARNING SURVEY.
  5. Click SUBMIT FORM once done.
  6. SMS, Messenger or Email notification will be sent to inform a successful enrollment or any lacking requirements.
  7. Once approved, applicants will automatically be included in the official list of enrollees
